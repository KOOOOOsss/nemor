About nemoré
Focused on jewelry, storage and travel with mindful luxury

Designed for jewelry lovers & frequent travellers

nemoré jewelry boxes focus on three core head words: jewelry, storage and travel. We create premium wooden boxes specially made for storing, organising and protecting precious jewellery while travelling.

Our target audience is men and women aged 25–50 who value fine accessories, neat storage and elegant travel essentials. The compact size, soft interior and built-in mirror make nemoré the perfect daily and travel companion for all jewelry enthusiasts.

Every nemoré jewelry box is hand-finished with sustainable wood and eco-friendly lining, combining durability, luxury and practicality for daily storage and travel needs.
