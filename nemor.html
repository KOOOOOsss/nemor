About NEMOR
Crafted for the discerning traveler

Heritage meets innovation

NEMOR was born from the desire to merge fine jewelry protection, watch safety and premium cosmetics storage. Our travel cases are designed together with Italian leather artisans and Swiss watch-case engineers to bring you the ultimate portable luxury.

Every detail — from soft anti-shock padding to custom gold-finish locks — ensures your most treasured items stay safe and beautifully organized across continents.

Sustainable luxury is at our core. We use ethically sourced leathers and recycled microfibre linings, and every box is hand-finished in limited numbers.
